### 🧠 Pathified — AI Career Guidance Platform
> For CS students who have no idea what to do with their degree (relatable)

- 25-question **adaptive quiz** — actually adapts, not just random shuffle
- **Groq API** for real-time AI-powered career recommendations
- Stack: `React` · `Supabase` · `EmailJS` · `Vercel`
- ✅ Fully deployed. Used by real humans.

### 🌱 TerraSense — Soil Analysis via NIR Spectroscopy
> Because someone had to put ML in the ground (literally)

- **PLSRegression** model predicting N, P, K, pH, moisture content
- `FastAPI` backend + `Groq`-powered crop-specific advisory layer
- Built for **Resurgence Hackathon @ LPU (CREST)**, extended post-competition
- Includes per-crop soil health scoring framework
